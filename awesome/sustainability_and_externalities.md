# Sustainability and Externalities

- [Tackling Climate Change with Machine Learning](https://arxiv.org/abs/1906.05433): maps climate applications where target-world outcomes differ from benchmark accuracy.
- [From Efficiency Gains to Rebound Effects](https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.16548): highlights why efficiency claims need downstream resource monitoring.
- [Green AI](https://arxiv.org/abs/1907.10597): supports reporting efficiency and compute alongside task performance.
- [Estimating the Carbon Footprint of BLOOM](https://jmlr.org/papers/v24/23-0069.html): provides a detailed example of emissions accounting for model development and deployment.
