# Benchmark Overclaiming

## Relevance

Benchmark scores become unsafe evidence when they are inflated into broad capability, safety, or deployment claims.

## Card Fields Strengthened

- `claim_boundaries`
- `target_world_state`
- `evaluator_frontier`

## Starter Resources

- [HELM: Holistic Evaluation of Language Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2211.09110): demonstrates multi-scenario, multi-metric reporting instead of a single universal score.

- Add examples where high benchmark performance fails to imply deployment readiness.
