# Leaderboard Validity

## Relevance

Leaderboards can encourage narrow optimization unless they report claim boundaries, uncertainty, and non-score constraints.

## Card Fields Strengthened

- `claim_type`
- `claim_boundaries`
- `verifier_stack`

## Starter Resources

- [BIG-bench](https://arxiv.org/abs/2206.04615): provides a broad benchmark suite while illustrating why heterogeneous aggregate scores need careful interpretation.
- [Measuring Massive Multitask Language Understanding](https://arxiv.org/abs/2009.03300): shows how broad benchmark coverage can still require careful claim boundaries.
- [Measuring Coding Challenge Competence With APPS](https://arxiv.org/abs/2105.09938): illustrates coding benchmark design and the limits of challenge-style scoring.
