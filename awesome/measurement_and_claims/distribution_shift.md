# Distribution Shift

## Relevance

Open-world evaluation must ask whether evidence collected under one distribution transfers to new users, tasks, environments, or deployment stages.

## Card Fields Strengthened

- `uncertainty`
- `target_world_state`
- `monitoring_and_rollback`

## Starter Resources

- [Dynabench](https://aclanthology.org/2021.naacl-main.324/): motivates dynamic evaluation when static datasets stop representing the frontier.
