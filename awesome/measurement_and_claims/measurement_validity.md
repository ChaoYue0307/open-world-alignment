# Measurement Validity

## Relevance

Before using a score as evidence, ask what construct the evaluation actually measures and which world-facing claim remains unsupported.

## Card Fields Strengthened

- `claim_type`
- `target_world_state`
- `claim_boundaries`

## Starter Resources

- [Evaluating Generative AI Systems Is a Social Science Measurement Challenge](https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.00561): frames generative AI evaluation as construct measurement rather than raw scoring.
- [CheckList](https://aclanthology.org/2020.acl-main.442/): demonstrates behavioral tests that make capability claims more specific.
