# Evaluating Beyond Expert Knowledge

## Relevance

Some systems may produce claims, proofs, designs, or discoveries that exceed current expert ability to judge directly.

## Card Fields Strengthened

- `evaluator_frontier`
- `verifier_stack`
- `uncertainty`

## Starter Resources

- [Superposition, Memorization, and Double Descent](https://transformer-circuits.pub/2023/toy-double-descent/index.html): illustrates how interpretability work can expose internal structure that surface evaluation misses.

## Open Questions

- How can external verifiers be built before experts understand the full solution?
- When should systems abstain from claims that exceed verifier capacity?
- How should reviewers score evidence for frontier capabilities?
