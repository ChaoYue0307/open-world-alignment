# Human-Signal Limits

- [Open Problems and Fundamental Limitations of RLHF](https://arxiv.org/abs/2307.15217): motivates explicit reporting of what human feedback can and cannot verify.
- [Training language models to follow instructions with human feedback](https://arxiv.org/abs/2203.02155): provides a baseline for preference and instruction-following signals.
- [TruthfulQA](https://aclanthology.org/2022.acl-long.229/): shows why plausible answers can fail truth-oriented evaluation.
- [Sycophancy to Subterfuge](https://arxiv.org/abs/2406.10162): illustrates how optimizing approval signals can create dangerous proxy failures.
