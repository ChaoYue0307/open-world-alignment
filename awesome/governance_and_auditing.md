# Governance and Auditing

- [NIST AI Risk Management Framework Playbook](https://airc.nist.gov/AI_RMF_Knowledge_Base/Playbook): helps translate card fields into governance actions and audit questions.
- [ISO/IEC 42001 overview](https://www.iso.org/standard/81230.html): gives an AI management-system frame for ownership and accountability fields.
- [Algorithmic Accountability Act archive](https://www.congress.gov/bill/117th-congress/house-bill/6580): motivates impact-assessment language for affected stakeholders and audit trails.
- [The Assessment List for Trustworthy AI](https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/library/assessment-list-trustworthy-artificial-intelligence-altai-self-assessment): provides a governance checklist precedent for reviewer-facing artifacts.
