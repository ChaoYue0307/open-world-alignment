# Beyond-Human Supervision

## Relevance

Some systems produce reasoning, discoveries, or plans that humans can request but cannot directly verify at instance level.

## Card Fields Strengthened

- `claim_type`
- `evaluator_frontier`
- `verifier_stack`

## Starter Resources

- [Eliciting Latent Knowledge](https://arxiv.org/abs/2212.03827): frames the difficulty of extracting truth from systems that may know more than supervisors can directly verify.

- Add resources on long proofs, autonomous science, large software changes, and long-horizon planning.
