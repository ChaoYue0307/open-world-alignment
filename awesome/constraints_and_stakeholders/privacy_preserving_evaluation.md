# Privacy-Preserving Evaluation

## Relevance

Open-world evaluation should not require exposing sensitive data or weakening privacy guarantees to measure performance.

## Card Fields Strengthened

- `hard_constraints`
- `evidence_bundle`
- `monitoring_and_rollback`

## Starter Resources

- [Differential Privacy](https://differentialprivacy.org/): provides foundations and community resources for privacy-preserving measurement.

## Expansion Targets

- Add private evaluation, secure aggregation, audit logging, and data-minimization resources.
