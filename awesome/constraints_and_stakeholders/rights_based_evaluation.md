# Rights-Based Evaluation

## Relevance

Some failures matter even if most users are satisfied; evaluation should preserve rights and contestability as hard constraints.

## Card Fields Strengthened

- `hard_constraints`
- `absent_stakeholders`
- `out_of_scope_uses`

## Starter Resources

- [EU Artificial Intelligence Act](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2024/1689/oj): provides a rights- and risk-oriented regulatory frame for AI system obligations.

## Expansion Targets

- Add rights, nondiscrimination, due process, contestability, and appeals-process resources.
