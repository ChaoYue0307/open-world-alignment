# Verifier-Stack Design

- [Let's Verify Step by Step](https://arxiv.org/abs/2305.20050): supports process-level verification for reasoning claims.
- [AlphaFold](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-021-03819-2): exemplifies scientific claims anchored in external structural evidence.
- [AlphaTensor](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-022-05172-4): shows algorithm discovery tied to executable and mathematical verification.
- [FunSearch](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-023-06924-6): illustrates search over programs with objective programmatic scoring.
