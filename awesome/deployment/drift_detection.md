# Drift Detection

## Relevance

Deployment distributions, users, data, and environments change, so evidence can become stale even when the card was initially valid.

## Card Fields Strengthened

- `uncertainty`
- `review_frequency`
- `deployment_signals_to_monitor`

## Starter Resources

- [Failing Loudly](https://arxiv.org/abs/1810.11953): studies methods for detecting dataset shift before silent performance degradation.

## Expansion Targets

- Add model drift, data drift, calibration drift, and environment-change monitoring resources.
