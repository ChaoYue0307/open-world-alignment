# Scalable Oversight

- [Weak-to-Strong Generalization](https://arxiv.org/abs/2312.09390): makes the evaluator frontier concrete when weaker supervisors guide stronger models.
- [Easy-to-Hard Generalization](https://arxiv.org/abs/2403.09472): studies when supervision transfers from easier tasks to harder tasks.
- [AI Safety via Debate](https://arxiv.org/abs/1805.00899): proposes adversarial assistance as an oversight mechanism for hard-to-judge claims.
- [Self-critiquing models for assisting human evaluators](https://arxiv.org/abs/2206.05802): helps separate assisted human review from independent verification.
