# Soft Verifiers

## Relevance

Soft verifiers such as expert review, model critique, and qualitative assessment add context but should not alone establish broad open-world claims.

## Card Fields Strengthened

- `soft_verifiers`
- `human_review_channels`
- `human_signal_cannot_verify`
