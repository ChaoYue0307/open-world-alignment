# Monitoring and Rollback

- [Release Strategies and the Social Impacts of Language Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/1908.09203): motivates staged deployment and monitored release.
- [Sociotechnical Safety Evaluation of Generative AI Systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2310.11986): surfaces deployment-context gaps that pre-release benchmarks miss.
- [AI Incident Database](https://incidentdatabase.ai/): provides incident vocabulary and examples for monitoring signals.
- [NIST AI Risk Management Framework](https://www.nist.gov/itl/ai-risk-management-framework): supports map-measure-manage-govern-monitor mappings for card fields.
