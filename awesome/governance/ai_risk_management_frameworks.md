# AI Risk Management Frameworks

## Relevance

Risk frameworks connect card evidence to organizational ownership, risk mapping, measurement, management, and governance.

## Card Fields Strengthened

- `risk_tier`
- `reviewers`
- `monitoring_and_rollback`

## Starter Resources

- [NIST AI RMF Playbook](https://airc.nist.gov/AI_RMF_Knowledge_Base/Playbook): provides operational prompts for applying AI risk-management functions.
- [The Assessment List for Trustworthy AI](https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/library/assessment-list-trustworthy-artificial-intelligence-altai-self-assessment): provides a governance checklist precedent for reviewer-facing artifacts.
