# Absent Stakeholders

- [Datasheets for Datasets](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/publication/datasheets-for-datasets/): establishes a precedent for standardized evidence artifacts and stakeholder-relevant documentation.
- [Data Statements for NLP](https://aclanthology.org/Q18-1041/): helps identify language communities and provenance limits absent from benchmark scores.
- [Model Cards for Model Reporting](https://arxiv.org/abs/1810.03993): informs intended-use, limitation, and evaluation reporting fields.
- [Dataset Nutrition Label](https://arxiv.org/abs/1805.03677): supports compact transparency artifacts for downstream users.
