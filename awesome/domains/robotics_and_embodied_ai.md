# Robotics and Embodied AI

## Relevance

Embodied systems affect physical spaces, so evaluation needs simulation, hardware evidence, safety interlocks, and incident monitoring.

## Card Fields Strengthened

- `external_tools_or_simulators`
- `hard_constraints`
- `monitoring_and_rollback`

## Starter Resources
