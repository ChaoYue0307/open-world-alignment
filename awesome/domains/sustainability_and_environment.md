# Sustainability and Environment

## Relevance

Environmental outcomes are physical, delayed, and distributed, so task performance must be connected to resource and impact accounting.

## Card Fields Strengthened

- `target_world_state`
- `absent_stakeholders`
- `hard_constraints`

## Starter Resources

- [Green AI](https://arxiv.org/abs/1907.10597): argues for reporting efficiency and resource costs alongside task performance.
- [Tackling Climate Change with Machine Learning](https://arxiv.org/abs/1906.05433): maps climate applications where target-world outcomes differ from benchmark accuracy.
- [From Efficiency Gains to Rebound Effects](https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.16548): highlights why efficiency claims need downstream resource monitoring.
- [Estimating the Carbon Footprint of BLOOM](https://jmlr.org/papers/v24/23-0069.html): provides detailed accounting for model training and deployment emissions.
