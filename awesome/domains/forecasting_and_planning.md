# Forecasting and Planning

## Relevance

Forecasts and plans are judged by future outcomes, calibration, causal assumptions, and decision consequences.

## Card Fields Strengthened

- `target_world_state`
- `uncertainty`
- `monitoring_and_rollback`

## Starter Resources

- [GraphCast](https://www.science.org/doi/10.1126/science.adi2336): illustrates forecasting quality anchored in out-of-sample forecast skill.
- [On Calibration of Modern Neural Networks](https://arxiv.org/abs/1706.04599): motivates calibration checks for uncertainty fields.
- [Selective Classification for Deep Neural Networks](https://arxiv.org/abs/1705.08500): supports abstention policies under uncertainty.
- [Conformal Prediction Under Covariate Shift](https://arxiv.org/abs/1904.06019): helps reason about uncertainty when deployment distributions shift.
- [Bayesian Uncertainty in Deep Learning](https://arxiv.org/abs/1506.02142): provides background for uncertainty reporting and model confidence limits.
