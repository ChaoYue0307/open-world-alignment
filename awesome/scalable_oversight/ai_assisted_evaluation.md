# AI-Assisted Evaluation

## Relevance

Humans may need retrieval, proof assistants, debuggers, simulators, or critique models to evaluate stronger systems.

## Card Fields Strengthened

- `required_tooling_for_human_review`
- `human_review_channels`
- `external_tools_or_simulators`

## Starter Resources

- [Self-critiquing models for assisting human evaluators](https://arxiv.org/abs/2206.05802): studies model assistance as support for human evaluation rather than as final evidence.
