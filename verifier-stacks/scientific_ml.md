# Scientific ML Verifier Stack

Use this stack when systems generate hypotheses, predictions, structures, simulations, or interventions whose value depends on empirical, mathematical, or physical validation.

## Human Signal

- Research-goal selection
- Hypothesis framing
- Experimental-design judgment
- Interpretation of evidence
- Domain-expert review

## World-Facing Verifiers

- Experimental validation
- Replication
- Simulation fidelity checks
- Physical-law consistency checks
- Theorem checking where applicable
- Out-of-sample predictive performance
- Calibration and uncertainty evaluation

## Hard Constraints

- Safety and biosafety limits
- Resource and compute limits
- No fabricated evidence
- No unsupported causal claim
- No deployment without domain-appropriate review

## Evaluator Frontier

Humans become weak supervisors when outputs require expensive experiments, specialized instrumentation, long-horizon validation, or mathematical/physical checks that cannot be performed from surface plausibility.

## Monitoring

- Replication outcomes
- Failed validation attempts
- Downstream experimental incidents
- Drift in predictive calibration
- Resource use
- Retractions, corrections, or audit findings
