# Code of Conduct

Participants are expected to keep discussion focused, respectful, and evidence-oriented.

## Expected Behavior

- Use concrete technical claims.
- Distinguish disagreement about evidence from personal criticism.
- Respect contributors working across research, engineering, governance, and audit contexts.
- Keep safety-sensitive examples high-level and evaluation-focused.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Promotional spam or low-signal link dumping.
- Attempts to add operationally harmful instructions under the guise of evaluation examples.
- Misrepresenting this toolkit as a safety certification.

Maintainers may remove issues, pull requests, or comments that violate these expectations.
