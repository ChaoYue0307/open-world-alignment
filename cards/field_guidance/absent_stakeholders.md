# Absent Stakeholders

## Purpose

This field identifies affected people, institutions, ecosystems, or future users who are not represented in the rating loop.

## Strong Answer

Relevant stakeholders include maintainers, downstream users, security teams, API consumers, release managers, and users affected by regressions.

## Weak Answer

Users are the stakeholders.

## Common Failure Mode

Stakeholder invisibility: the people who rate the system differ from the people who bear consequences.

## Reviewer Question

Who is affected but does not directly provide feedback?

## Coding-Agent Example

Downstream users affected by regressions and future maintainers bearing maintenance burden.

## Foundation-Model Example

People whose data appears in context, people affected by user decisions, and communities affected by misinformation or misuse.
