# Human-Signal Role

## Purpose

This field explains which human-facing signals are used and what they cannot verify unaided.

## Strong Answer

Human review assesses intent, readability, maintainability, and contextual fit, but does not replace executable tests, hidden tests, static analysis, or deployment monitoring.

## Weak Answer

Humans liked the outputs.

## Common Failure Mode

Human-signal monism: labels, preferences, ratings, or judge models become the master evidence channel.

## Reviewer Question

Could the system receive high human approval while failing the target world state?

## Coding-Agent Example

Maintainer comments help interpret the task, but cannot verify hidden regressions unaided.

## Foundation-Model Example

Preference ratings assess helpfulness and clarity, but cannot verify factuality, privacy leakage, or misuse pathways alone.
