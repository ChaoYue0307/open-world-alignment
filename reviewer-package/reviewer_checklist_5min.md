# 5-Minute Reviewer Checklist

Use this without reading implementation docs.

## Quick Pass

- Is the central claim specific enough to be falsified or narrowed?
- Does the card say what the benchmark or evaluation does not establish?
- Is there at least one hard verifier tied to the target-world claim?
- Are human signals described with explicit limits?
- Are rollback triggers and revision authority named?

## Immediate Concern

Ask for claim narrowing or revision if any answer is "no" and the paper makes a beyond-human, deployment, safety, or ASI-relevant claim.

## Minimal Reviewer Note

"The evidence supports the reported metric, but the card does not yet establish the broader open-world claim because unsupported claims, verifier independence, and rollback authority are underspecified."
